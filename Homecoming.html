Homecoming.

Homecoming, subtitled "A Film by Beyoncé", is a 2019 concert film about American singer Beyoncé and her performance at the 2018 Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival, written, executive produced and directed by Beyoncé herselfIt was with this one word, pasted across a yellow background that Netflix sparked excitement on April 7, 2019. It didn’t take long for Twitter users to join the dots. Headlining at Coachella in 2018, Beyoncé lit up the stage in a vibrant yellow Balmain ensemble, stitched with Greek mythological references, the font identical to that in the post shared by Netflix on the social network. Homecoming is also the name of the clothing line launched by the artist. On April 17, the documentary Homecoming, devoted to Beyoncé's electric performance at Coachella 2018 arrived on Netflix. Over two hours long, it gives a priveliged glimpse into backstage of one of the biggest shows in the festival's history. From her sporty preparation, to intimate moments with her family, Beyoncé hides nothing.
